What is Caseware Audit?

Caseware Audit is a cloud-based audit solution that provides an automated and guided workflow through a Canadian audit engagement. The auditor's responses to various checklists intelligently populate the client file with relevant documents and content for planning, risk assessment and risk response.

Caseware Audit's content and workflows follow CPA Canada's PEG guidelines.
