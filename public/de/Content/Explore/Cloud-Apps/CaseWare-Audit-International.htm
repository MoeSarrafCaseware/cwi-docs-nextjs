Was ist Caseware Audit International?

Caseware Audit International is a pure cloud-based solution that provides an automated and guided workflow for an audit engagement following International Standards on Auditing (ISAs). The auditor’s responses to various checklists intelligently populates the client file with relevant documents and content for planning, risk assessment and fieldwork.
