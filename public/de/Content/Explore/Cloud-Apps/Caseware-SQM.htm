What is Caseware SQM?

Caseware SQM (SQM) is an intuitive cloud-based solution that provides you with an efficient solution to design, operate, monitor and evaluate your system of quality management.

SQM ensures that you complete your firm and engagement-level work in accordance with the Canadian Standard on Quality Management (CSQM) 1, Quality Management for Firms that Perform Audits or Review of Financial Statements, or Other Assurance or Related Services Engagements and Canadian Standard on Quality Management (CSQM) 2, Engagement Quality Reviews.

While working in SQM, you may find that there are three main roles that will help streamline your workflow:

  • System designers - Staff members involved with building the SQM.

  • System monitors and evaluators - Staff members dedicated to providing maintenance or process improvements.

  • End users - Staff members dedicated to completing or reviewing work.
