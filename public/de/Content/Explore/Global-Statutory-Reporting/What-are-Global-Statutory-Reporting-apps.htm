What are Global Statutory Reporting apps

Global Statutory Reporting apps are global Caseware Cloud solutions designed to simplify the process of generating statutory reports, while ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

The apps feature tools for financial statement preparation and offer a guided workflow that's tailored based on the engagement being performed. Documents and content automatically populate in the engagement file based on the responses provided in checklists and the client's financial data. The tailored checklists are designed to allow you to include disclosures aligned with the local regulatory framework, as well as region-specific requirements, such as presentation languages and relevant disclosures.
