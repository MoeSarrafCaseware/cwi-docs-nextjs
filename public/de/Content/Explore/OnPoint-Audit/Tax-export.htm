Tax export codes

In the 9-100 Tax export form, you can export your tax year’s data, then import it to your third-party tax vendor. OnPoint Audit supports various tax software and export codes.

Tax software

The OnPoint Audit tax export supports the 2023 tax year for the following tax software:

  • CCH Axcess™ Tax

  • CCH ProSystem fx® Tax

  • Lacerte® Tax

  • UltraTax® CS
