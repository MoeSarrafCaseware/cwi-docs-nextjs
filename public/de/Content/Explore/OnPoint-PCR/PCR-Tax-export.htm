Tax export codes

In the 6-100 Tax report form, you can export your tax year's data, then import it into your third-party tax vendor. OnPoint PCR supports various tax software and export codes.

Tax software

The OnPoint PCR tax export supports the 2022 tax year for the following tax software:

  • Lacerte® Tax
  • CCH Axcess™ Tax
  • CCH ProSystem fx® Tax
  • UltraTax® CS
