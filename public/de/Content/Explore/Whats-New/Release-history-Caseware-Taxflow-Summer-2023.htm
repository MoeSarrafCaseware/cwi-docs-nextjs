What's new - Caseware Taxflow Summer 2023 Release (v.2.210.0)

Here is what’s new in the Caseware Taxflow Summer 2023 release.

Partnerships support for 2022 tax exports

In addition to C-Corporation and S-Corporation support, Caseware Taxflow now supports 2022 tax exports for CCH (Axcess / Prosystem fx) for Partnerships.

Interactive welcome guide

To provide you with a seamless onboarding experience, a new three-minute welcome tour now automatically displays to introduce the key areas of a Caseware Taxflow engagement. The tour includes screenshots, gifs and links to additional online help topics to help you effectively navigate and perform your engagement.

The welcome guide can be accessed at any time from the Resource Center.

Fixes

The following list features a brief description of the issues that have been resolved in this release.

  • Fixed an issue in the Tax groups assignment page where the text was not properly displayed when the table was resized.

  • Fixed an issue where queries documents were not properly displaying their content when accessed by contacts.

  • Fixed an issue in Schedule K where the Final tax balance was multiplied by 10 incorrectly.
