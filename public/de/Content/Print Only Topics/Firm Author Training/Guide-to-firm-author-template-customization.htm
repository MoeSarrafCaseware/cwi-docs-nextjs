Introduction to Firm Author Template Customization

This guide is to outline the Firm Author’s ability to customize content created by the Primary Author or Firm Author at the Template level.

Firm Author Code

Beschreibung

FPA

The Firm Author has the ability to customize a given option on their own content and content created by the Primary Author.

FA

The Firm Author has the ability to customize a given option on their own content. The Firm author cannot customize content created by the Primary Author.

NA

 

The functionality or content is not customizable by the Firm Author or a given option is not available for the Firm Author to control on their own content or Primary Author content.

Notes

Firm Authors can then choose to Suppress the content created by the Primary Author in the template and show the newly created content.

Firm Authors have the ability in checklists, memos, letters, and the financial statements, to copy text areas or dynamic tables created by the Primary Author. Firm Authors can then paste the copied items in a new section, which retains any formulas or placeholders, and then can modify the content.

In addition to the ability to copy the text and dynamic tables, Firm Authors can view and copy visibility conditions set by the Primary Author in the financial statements.
