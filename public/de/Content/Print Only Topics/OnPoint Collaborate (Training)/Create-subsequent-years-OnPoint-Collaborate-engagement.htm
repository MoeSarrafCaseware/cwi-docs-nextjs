Create subsequent years OnPoint Collaborate engagement

When it's time to create next year's OnPoint Collaborate engagement, you can carry forward the engagement. Carry forward is a time-saving utility that allows you to re-use information, as you can accept all of last year's changes or only work on the individual items that have changed.

To carry forward the engagement:

  1. From the Cloud menu (), select OnPoint Collaborate to open the engagement view.

  2. Select your engagement to open the side panel on the right, then select Create Next Period's File.

  3. In the OnPoint Collaborate dialog, choose ACME Inc. as your Entity and enter 2020 - ACME Inc - <your initials> as the Name for the engagement file.

  4. In the Preparer field, enter your first name to search for your account. Select your account to assign yourself as the preparer.

  5. In the Reviewer field, enter part of another staff member's name. Select the account to assign the staff member as the reviewer.

  6. At the bottom of the OnPoint Collaborate dialog, select Save.

The new engagement file opens.
