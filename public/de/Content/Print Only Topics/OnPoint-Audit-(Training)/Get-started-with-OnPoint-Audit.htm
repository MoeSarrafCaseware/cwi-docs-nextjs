Get started with OnPoint Audit - Staff

Before you get started with OnPoint Audit, ensure that your firm administrator completes the necessary prerequisites in Get started with OnPoint Audit—Firm administrator.

  1. Engagement beginnen

  2. Complete the engagement setup procedures

  3. „Acceptance and Continuance“ (Annahme und Fortführung) Prozesse durchführen

  4. Review and send the engagement letter

  5. Risikobeurteilung durchführen

  6. Import the client's data

  7. Complete the materiality form

  8. Feldforschung durchführen

  9. Engagement abschließen

  10. Archive and lock down the engagement
