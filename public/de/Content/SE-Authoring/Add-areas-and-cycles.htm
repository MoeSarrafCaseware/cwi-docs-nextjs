Add areas and cycles

Business cycles represent the operation that initiates, records, processes, and approves transactions and account balance or disclosure entries. Once you create business cycles, they can be attached to trial balance groups.

Prüffelder erlauben Ihnen, Jahresabschlussgruppen auf einer höheren Ebene zur Risikoidentifikation und -bewertung zusammenzufassen (oftmals auf der Ebene des Prüfungsprogramms). Sie ermöglichen Ihnen auch, Elemente in einer anderen Reihenfolge als die in der Standardpositionenstruktur der Hauptabschlussübersicht verwendete zu gruppieren.

Hinweis: Make sure areas and cycles are enabled in the product.json file, otherwise, the Cycles and Areas tabs will not be available. To learn more see Enable or disable features in your product.

To add a business cycle:

  1. Go to Features | Cycles.

  2. Select + Business Cycle.

  3. Enter the name of the new cycle.

    4. You can use the arrow buttons at the end of the line to rearrange cycles. You can also remove a cycle by selecting ().

To add an area:

  1. Go to Features | Areas.

  2. Select + Areas, then enter the name of the new area.

    3. You can use the arrow buttons at the end of the line to rearrange areas. You can also remove an area by selecting ().
