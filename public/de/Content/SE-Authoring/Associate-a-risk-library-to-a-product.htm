Associate a risk library to a product

If you have published a risk library, you can associate it to a product. Users can then select the applicable and relevant risks from the library and add them to engagements.

Bitte beachten Sie:

  • The new risks module must be enabled in the product that you want to associate a risk library to.

  • A product can only have one published risk library version associated to it at a time, however, you can associate a published risk library version to multiple products.

    For example, you can create two risk libraries, Risk Library A and Risk Library B, and publish versions for them. You can then choose only one of those libraries to associate to Product A.

    If you select Risk Library A, you can't associate Risk Library B to Product A, however you can associate Risk Library A to your other products, Product B and Product C.

To start, select Settings from the Cloud menu(), then select Risk Library | Manage Risk Library.

To link a risk library to a product:

  1. Select the risk library from the left pane.

  2. Select the Copy () icon to copy the internal ID of the library.

  3. Open your product template.

  4. Select the Information icon () at the top menu, then choose Settings to open the Product Settings dialog.

  5. Select Risks | General.

  6. Go to the Library Management tab and paste the Internal ID of the library.

  7. Select the Published Version you want to link from the drop-down.

You can view the risk library and version associated to your product in the About dialog.
