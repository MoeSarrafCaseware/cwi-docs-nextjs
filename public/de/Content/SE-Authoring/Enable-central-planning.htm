Enable central planning

Central planning allows users to sync risks, controls and selected checklists across engagements so the work doesn’t need to be duplicated in multiple engagements. When users set an engagement as a central planning engagement, all risks, controls, and selected checklists are synced to the destination engagements.

To enable central planning in your product, select the Information icon () at the top menu and choose Settings to open the Product Settings dialog. On the Central Planning tab, select Enable Central Planning. You can then select which checklist documents in your template are synced. All risks and controls are synced by default.

To learn how users create and manage central planning engagement, see Set up central planning.
