Firm author

Firm authors are administrators within a firm who customize the product templates provided by the primary author. This is often done to add firm-specific branding and methodology to the product.

To learn more about setting up a firm author role in your SE product, see Manage the firm template.

Goal: Customize the default product content to meet specific firm needs. Unternehmensanpassungen umfassen das Hinzufügen von Inhalt, das Einrichten eines Firmenlogos und das Ändern oder Unterdrücken des Standardinhalts. To learn more about what firm authors can customize, see the topics under Practice > Templates and Authoring in the end user help.
