Primary author

Primary authors are distributor development teams product owners with industry knowledge, who create SE products that allow users in their market to perform engagements in accordance with jurisdictional standards.

Goal: Create and set up an SE product, author the product template, perform the necessary development tasks and distribute the product.

Product Manager

Product managers are responsible for the development of products for an organization. Product managers own the business strategy behind a product, specify the functional requirements, and generally manage the launch of features.

Role highlights:

  • Develop the short-term and long-term product roadmap (decide what to build now, what to build later and what not to build).
  • Develop epics and review user stories, product requirements documents and wireframes.
  • Constantly communicate across all organizational functions to make sure that everyone is moving at the same pace and working towards the same goal.

Content Developer

Content Developers author templates according to requirements provided by the product manager.

Role highlights:

  • Handle content authoring and product configuration aspects.
  • Review client feedback, resolve bugs and post feature requests.
  • Review the template changes from Caseware and evaluate how they affect the existing product template or how they can be leveraged to improve them.

Solution Developer

Solution Developers perform solution customization tasks as agreed on with the product manager. They have working coding experience so that can follow samples and create some custom configurations using javascript, html, XML or JSON files, but require some assistance from Caseware to publish their configurations.

Role highlights:

  • Handle all coding work that's done on top of the existing Out Of The Box Cloud Engagements code to customize the solution per market requirements.
  • Use Caseware International scripting APIs to write scripts that help advance the intelligence of the product templates.
  • Troubleshoot issues encountered by internal teams.

Forms Developer

Form Developers have experience building custom solutions with the SE Builder platform and are able to be a little more "hacky" if needed. For example, they can develop a microservice to pull data from an external source into Cloud.

Role highlights:

  • Develop a custom product for their market based on SE Builder.
  • Collaborate and coordinate with Third Party Solutions (TPS) and SE product team.
