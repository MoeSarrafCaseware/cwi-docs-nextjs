Request a product

To request a product, clone the the JIRA ticket DIST-1925 .

Caseware International (CWI) notifies you once your SE product is accessible.

Note the following when you name your SE product:

  • Do not use "Cloud" in the product name. Your marketing material can describe the product as being cloud-based but should not use the term "Cloud" in the product name.

  • The product must have a unique name to avoid confusion in marketing and online documentation for each product or application. For example, you can't name your SE product "Audit". The name must include a unique word to distinguish it from other SE Audit products, such as, "Audit - India", or "Audit - NPO".

What you need to provide to CWI

Make sure you copy the content in the description of the DIST-1925 ticket and complete as much of the information as possible before you submit your ticket.

The following list summarizes the information that you need to provide to CWI including a brief description and examples.

  • Beschreibung

    A brief description of your product.

    Beispiel: We want to have a new audit product based on a new audit methodology as this will allow us to get into a wider market in our market.

  • Key Dates

    Provide the following key dates for your project:

    • Development Start Date
    • Client Beta Release Date
    • Release to Production

  • Do you require a kick off meeting?

    Indicate whether or not you require a kick off meeting. Die folgenden Optionen stehen zur Verfügung:

    • Yes, we do not have any experience with cloud development and require new cloud development sites.
    • Yes, we would like to present the solution that we are build and review our gap analysis.
    • No, thanks.

  • Gap analysis

    Provide a gap analysis of the Caseware Platforms that are required for your product.

  • Business Case

    Provide a brief reason for the product. Include the size of the potential market and a workflow that users of a minimum viable product follow.

  • Product Manager

    Name and email of product manager.

    Beispiel: Jane Smith, <jane.smith@caseware.com>

  • Lead Developer

    Name and email of lead product developer.

    Beispiel: John Smith, <john.smith@caseware.com>

  • Typ

    Select the product type. Die folgenden Optionen stehen zur Verfügung:

    • Smart Engagement Template (e)
    • Collaborate Plugin (p)
    • Microservice (m)

    Beispiel: Smart Engagement Template (e)

  • Product Name

    A name for your product.

    Beispiel: SDK Demo Engagement

  • Development Firm

    Provide the URL for your development firm.

    Example: http://ca.cwcloudpartner.com/ca-develop

  • Base SE template

    Provide a base template for the new product to copy from if applicable.

    If this isn't possible we will use the current TPS Generic Engagement (com.caseware.tps.e.generic) as the Base Template.
