Cloud Engagements authoring

Welcome to Cloud Engagements authoring, where we describe concepts, workflows and tasks necessary for the design and distribution of Cloud Engagement products (also known as, Smart Engagements or SE).

Engagement products can be applications for performing audits, review and compilation, or other types of engagements related to assurance, tax and compliance. As a Caseware International authorized primary author, it is your responsibility to complete the necessary setup in Caseware Cloud. Refer to Getting Started with Cloud for more information.
