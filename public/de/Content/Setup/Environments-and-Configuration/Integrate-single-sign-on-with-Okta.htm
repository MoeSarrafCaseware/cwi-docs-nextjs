Einmaliges Anmelden in Okta integrieren

Before you integrate single sign-on with Okta, ensure that:

  • Einmaliges Anmelden für Ihre Firma aktivieren.

  • You have administrator access to your firm's Okta account.

We recommend that single sign-on integration is performed by an administrator who is familiar with single sign-on setup in Okta.
