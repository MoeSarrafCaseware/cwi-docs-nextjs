What is Caseware AiDA?

Caseware AiDA is an AI-powered digital assistant designed to enhance efficiency and provide dynamic, context-aware responses to profession-specific enquiries.

When AiDA is active, it can provide context and summarize lengthy documents. It automatically focuses on the current document you are viewing, allowing you to jump between documents and continue to receive relevant answers quickly.

For information on using AiDA, see Get started with Caseware AiDA.
