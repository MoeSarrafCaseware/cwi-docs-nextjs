Identify preliminary analytical procedures

Use the 501 Preliminary analytical procedures document to identify relationships (for possible use in substantive analytical procedures), risks, inconsistencies as well unusual transactions, events, amounts and ratios or trends that will likely require an audit response.

The analysis charts in this document are linked to data throughout the engagement to help you visually compare data and discover trends and patterns using predefined data analytics. Use the drop-down menu to select Planning, Preliminary or Final results. Use the filter switches to display the desired analyses figures.
