Subscribed items

From My Settings | Notifications | Subscribed Items, you can view and update your subscription status for individual content. Subscribed content is displayed with hyperlinks to the content.

You are automatically subscribed to all content that you have added to Cloud. An email notification is sent whenever modifications are made to your content. For example, if a user comments on an activity that you posted, you will be notified by email.

Email notifications are not sent to you for updates you make.

Number Name Description
Remove Unsubscribes you from the selected content.
Subscribed items list The list of content you are currently subscribed to.
