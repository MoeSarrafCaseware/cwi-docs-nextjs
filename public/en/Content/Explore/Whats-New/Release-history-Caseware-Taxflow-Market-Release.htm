What's new - Caseware Taxflow Market Release (v.2.200.0)

Here is what’s new in the Caseware Taxflow market release.

S-Corporation support for 2022 tax exports

In addition to C-Corporation support, Caseware Taxflow now supports 2022 tax exports for CCH (Axcess / Prosystem fx) for S-Corporations.

Reclassifying adjustments for Schedule L

In the Tax adjustments form, you can now post tax reclassifying adjustments for Schedule L - Balance Sheet. To learn more, see Tax adjustments.

For C-Corporations, the Schedule L - Balance Sheet layout now includes any tax reclassifying adjustments that have been posted.
