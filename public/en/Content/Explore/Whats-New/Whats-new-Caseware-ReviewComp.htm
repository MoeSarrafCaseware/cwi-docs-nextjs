What's new - Caseware ReviewComp Spring 2025 (v 1.2985.0)

Here's a list of what's new in the Caseware ReviewComp Spring 2025 release.

New features and enhancements

Not-for-profit workflow revision

Understanding the entity

2-05-2 Understanding the entity - Basics has been renamed to Understanding the entity and 2-05-3 Understanding the entity – Systems has been removed as it is not part of the alternative PEG NFP review forms.

Note: Procedures related to 2-05-3 Understanding the entity – Systems are included in the Fieldwork phase.

Intangibles and goodwill

3-W Intangible assets and goodwill has been split into 3-W Intangible assets and 3-Y Goodwill and 3.W Intangible assets and goodwill has been split into 3.W Intangible assets and 3.Y Goodwill. 3-Y Goodwill and 3.Y Goodwill are only available in for-profit engagements.

Note disclosures for not-for-profit engagements

We have added additional note disclosures specific to not-for-profit engagements:

  • Purpose of the organization

  • Basis of presentation

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Basis of presentation

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Fund accounting

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Revenue recognition – Restricted fund method

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Revenue recognition – Deferral method

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Contributed services

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Donated goods

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Property, plant and equipment

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Callable debt

  • Summary of significant accounting policies: Measurement uncertainty
