What's new - Cloud 40.0

Find out what's new in Caseware Cloud 40.0.

Performance enhancements

This release includes several enhancements that align with our ongoing commitment to improve Cloud performance.

Deprecation of Google & SharePoint integration

Due to the low usage of Google Drive and Microsoft SharePoint integration capabilities, these features will no longer be supported with this release. This change enables us to focus our efforts on solutions that provide greater value and long-term benefits to our entire user base.

For more information, including how this may impact your workflow, see Deprecation of Google & SharePoint integration.
