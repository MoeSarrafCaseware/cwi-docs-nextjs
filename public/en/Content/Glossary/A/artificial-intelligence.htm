artificial intelligence

The field of computer science focused on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks could include:

  • Reasoning: Applying logic and knowledge to solve problems and make decisions.

  • Perception: Interpreting sensory inputs, such as visual, auditory, and other data.

  • Language Understanding: Comprehending and generating human language.

  • Learning: Acquiring new information and skills through experience and data.

  • Planning: Setting and achieving goals.

  • Robotics: Controlling and interacting with physical devices.

Various statutes and regulations may define AI more specifically beyond this general definition. Consult statutes and regulations as needed in your jurisdiction.
