Perform a Caseware ReviewComp tax engagement

Caseware ReviewComp (CW ReviewComp) lets you perform a complete corporate tax return directly from Caseware Cloud. In addition to all standard federal and provincial forms, CW ReviewComp contains worksheets specifically designed to streamline the process of completing the return. CW ReviewComp is designed and developed in partnership with the Canadian Revenue Agency (CRA) to ensure compliance, and links up with the CRA's Auto-fill service to import up to five prior years' tax data. When you've completed the return, you can access the CRA's EFILE service from the ReviewComp interface, or you can print a hard copy for the client to review and file themselves.

Caseware ReviewComp is also certified for the preparation of corporate tax. To learn more, see Corporate tax certification updates.
