Why use OnPoint PCR?

Let's take a look at the main features of OnPoint PCR.

Financial Statement Reference Copy creation assistance

What it does:

  • Generates reference copies of your client's financial statements

  • Automatically imports information and abstracts into a financial statement format

  • Optimizes your effort in assisting with your clients' financial statement creation.

Benefits:

  • Efficient drafting of financial statements due to the automatic link to the relevant client data, from an imported trial balance for instance

  • Included analytics tools that are driven directly from financial statement entries

Guided Client Collaboration

What it provides:

  • Client collaboration prompts as needed during an engagement

  • Direct client procedure to "binder" links

  • Direct import of financial data from top packages like QuickBooks, Xero, and Excel

Benefit:

  • Generates accurate engagement letters for your clients using our four-step guided onboarding tool

  • Simplifies client collaboration by monitoring your engagement progression and providing prompts whenever communication with your client is needed, all of which is handled from within the system

  • Eliminates import and translation steps by inserting client financial trial balances into the correct audit or review documentation
