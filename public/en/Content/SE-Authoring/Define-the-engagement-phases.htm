Define the engagement phases

Phases represent the different stages of the engagement. For example, an audit engagement might include a Planning and Risk Assessment phase, a Fieldwork phase, and a Completion phase. Once you create phases, you can organize new documents in the Documents page by the engagement phase they belong to.

To add a phase:

  1. Go to Features | Phases.

  2. Select + Phase, then enter the name of the new phase.

    3. You can use the arrow buttons at the end of the line to rearrange phases. You can also remove a phase by selecting ().
