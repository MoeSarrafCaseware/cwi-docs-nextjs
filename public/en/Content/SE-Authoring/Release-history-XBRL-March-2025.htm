What’s new in XBRL - March 2025

The following lists describe the improvements and fixed issues in the March 2025 XBRL updates.

SE Client

  • [SE-52972] - Made technical improvements to the creation and modification of custom numerics.

  • [SE-52913] [SE-52911] - Made technical improvements to the creation and modification of custom contexts.

  • [SE-52377] - Fixed a validation error that prevented tag application in new custom contexts.

Microservices

XBRL Server (Ver 2.5.4.1)
