What is multi-language support

Multi-language support aims at facilitating SE product offerings in regions and markets where there is a requirement to present deliverables and content in multiple languages.

With multi-language support, you can work in one SE app that meets the language requirements of your market, instead of previously, having to create and maintain an app for each language.

For engagement preparers, multi-language support provides the flexibility of changing the content and deliverables language from within the engagement file. This way, they can efficiently author or make content changes.
