Definir las funciones de aprobación

Note: Custom sign-off roles and schemes are only available if they have been enabled in your product.

Custom sign-off roles allow you to define custom labels for preparer and reviewer roles. In addition to the predefined roles available, you can add as many roles as needed to meet the specific requirements of your firm.

For example, you can define Jr. Preparer and Sr. Preparer as preparer roles and Reviewer, Partner, ECQR as reviewer roles.

Once you define custom roles, you can create custom sign-off schemes to track the required reviewer and preparer workflows on a document. See Set up signoff schemes for more information.
