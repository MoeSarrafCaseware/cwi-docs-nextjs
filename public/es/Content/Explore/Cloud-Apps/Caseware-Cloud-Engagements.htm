¿Qué es Caseware Cloud Connector?

Caseware Cloud Engagements is the underlying platform that your engagement products are built on. Some updates to your product come from the product itself, while some are a result of updates made to the Caseware Cloud Engagements platform.

When you want to learn about new features and updates that affect your engagement, make sure you check both the release notes for your product as well as What's new in Caseware Cloud Engagements.
