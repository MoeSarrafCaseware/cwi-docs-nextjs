Personal

Staff are managers, partners, junior or senior staff accountants or auditors, etc. who use Caseware Cloud Apps to perform audit, review and compilation or other types of engagements related to assurance, financial reporting, tax and compliance.

Staff members typically take on the preparer, reviewer and second reviewer roles in cloud engagements.

Staff roles in a small-size firm

The following staff roles can be available in a small-size firm of 1 to 5 members.

  • Manager Partner
  • Senior Accountant
  • cuenta

Staff roles in a large-size firm

The following staff roles can be available in a large-size firm of over 5 members.

  • Manager Partner
  • Engagement Partner
  • Engagement Quality Review (EQR)
  • Senior Manager
  • Audit Manager
  • Senior Accountant
  • cuenta
