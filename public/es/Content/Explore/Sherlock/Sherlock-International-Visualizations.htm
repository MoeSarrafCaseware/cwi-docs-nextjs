Visualizaciones de Sherlock International

Users of the Audit International template for Caseware Working Papers can now gain more insight into their audit data with easy-to-use Sherlock International Visualizations. This product includes predesigned visualizations that automatically populate with the audit data from an engagement file, including:

  • Riesgos y controles
  • Información financiera
  • Resumen de socios y gerentes

For information on licensing Sherlock International Visualizations, contact your local distributor.
