Ver una visualización

Use the Sherlock Reader app to view and customize the visualizations that are available to your account. The icon preceding the visualization name indicates whether the visualization is purchased () or shared ().

Notas:

  • You can only view visualizations for which your firm has purchased licenses through MyCaseware, or visualizations that have been shared with you by other staff through Sherlock Builder.

  • You can only view data from entities where you have the Viewer permissionViewer permission.
