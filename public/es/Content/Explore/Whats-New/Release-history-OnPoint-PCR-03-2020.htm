What's new - OnPoint PCR 03-2020

The tax export feature in OnPoint PCR has been improved and expanded.

  • OnPoint PCR now provides tax export functionality that's compatible with Thomson Reuters UltraTax CS®. We've also expanded the feature to address filings for the 2019 tax year and enhanced how it handles adjustments that have been posted prior to performing the tax export.

  • Tax export supports corporations, S corporations and partnerships. It also includes common tax forms related to each entity type. In addition to Thomson Reuters UltraTax CS®, OnPoint PCR is also compatible with Intuit's Lacerte© Tax, CCH® ProSystem fx® Tax, and CCH Axcess™ Tax.
