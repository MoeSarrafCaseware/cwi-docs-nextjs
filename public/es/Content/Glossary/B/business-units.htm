unidades empresariales

  1. In the context of Caseware Cloud, a method of structuring multiple entities belonging to the same customer organization to interconnect their Caseware Cloud sites. As a result, staff can work with content across multiple interconnected Cloud sites, across all the sites where staff have access.

  2. In the context of general business, smaller segments such as divisions or teams, making up an entity. In some Caseware solutions, entities are segmented into business units.
