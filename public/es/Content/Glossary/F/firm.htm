A group of accounting professionals who provide clients with financial services. In the context of Caseware Cloud, firm is for most intents and purposes a synonym of organization: a company that provides these financial services through a firm name assigned by Caseware and which is part of a URL the firm gives to its users to be able to log in to the Caseware Cloud apps the firm provides. See the example of a firm name below in a sample Caseware Cloud URL.

https://<region>.casewarecloud.com/<FirmName>/e/eng/<engagement-ID>/index.jsp#
