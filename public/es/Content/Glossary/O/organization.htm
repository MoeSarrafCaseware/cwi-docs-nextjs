organización

The name of the company providing services to its clients through Caseware Cloud apps. Generally, an organization is an accounting firm and can be considered a B2B client of Caseware. Organization is listed as a field in the firm settings defining a firm. Firm can be considered a synonym for most purposes. However, in the Caseware Cloud context, it refers to the Caseware Cloud site assigned to the organization providing services to its clients through Caseware Cloud apps. Many firms or Caseware Cloud sites can belong to the same organization. See business units for more information.

See also firm.
