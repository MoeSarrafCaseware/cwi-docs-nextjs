Functionality not supported by Caseware ReviewComp

CRA filing functionality

Caseware ReviewComp does not support Corporation Internet Filing for the following corporation types:

  • Income Tax Regulation 403 - Insurance corporations

  • Income Tax Regulation 404 - Banks

  • Income Tax Regulation 405 - Trust and loan corporations

  • Income Tax Regulation 412 - Divided businesses

Additionally, Caseware ReviewComp does not support the CRA's Attach-a-doc Efile service.
