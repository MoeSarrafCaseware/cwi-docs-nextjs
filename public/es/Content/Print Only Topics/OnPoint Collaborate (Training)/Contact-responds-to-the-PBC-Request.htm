Contact responds to the PBC Request

Once you have shared the document, your contacts can see the query on the My Work pane when they log in to Cloud. They can select the document to open it and provide responses. In this chapter, we're going to go through how a client responds to a query.

Open the link you've received in a separate browser, such as Firefox or Microsoft Edge, and sign in to Cloud as your contact persona. The query you sent is displayed in the My Work pane.
