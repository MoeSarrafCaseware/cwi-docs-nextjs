What is OnPoint Collaborate

Collaborate is part of the OnPoint Suite that solely addresses client collaboration. Client collaboration surpasses the team work concept. It refers to how accounting firms:

  • Communicate with clients to request information

  • Keep track of client activity

  • Simply and effectively provide and receive feedback

  • Perform all the above within a secure hub

OnPoint Collaborate takes collaborative accounting to the next level. It enables firms to serve clients with speed and efficiency, offering immediate response to questions and exchange of data and documents - all within the secure Caseware Cloud platform.

About this guide

The practice aids in this guide have been designed for a small- and medium-sized non-public company. They do not address the wide range of circumstances or specific industry issues that may exist for a particular client or groups of clients (such as those operating in a specialized industry, government or non-profit organization or complex IT environments). Consequently, the forms should always be tailored to address the known facts and circumstances of each engagement.
