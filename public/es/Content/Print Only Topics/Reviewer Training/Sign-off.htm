Aprobación

When you have finished reviewing a document, you can sign off on it.

Sign off on procedures

In checklists, you can sign-off on individual procedures and the conclusion if this option has been enabled in the checklist settings.

To sign off on procedures, select the sign-off icon next to the procedure.

To sign-off on the conclusion, select the Sign off button below the conclusions section.

¿Desea aprobar este documento?

You can sign off on documents directly from the Documents page. Each document has a sign-off icon that indicates its status:

  • No aprobadoNo aprobado

  • Preparadosign off prepared icon

  • Revisadosign off reviewed icon

Select the sign-off icon to review when the document was signed off, to sign off on a role, or to remove a prior sign-off.
