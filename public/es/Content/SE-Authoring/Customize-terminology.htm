Personalizar terminología:

You can customize the Preliminary, Final and Previous terms, which are used by default in the Analysis tab in checklists and the trial balance page to label the raw data that users have imported and the final data (post adjustments, rounding, etc.). You can also customize the full and abbreviated versions of the Risk term, to determine how risks are referred to everywhere in your product.

Go to Product Settings | Features | Terminology and enter the terms that you prefer to use next to Prelim. Final, Previous, Risk and Risk Abbreviation. The new terms immediately apply in your product.

Note that if you have enabled planning balances, you can also customize the terminology for Planning.
