Set up the statement of Cash Flows

You can set up the statement of Cash Flow using your SE product API. For details on how to set the Cash Flow statement, contact the SE Product team.

Once you set up the statement of Cash Flow, it's available in the financial statements document and you can add it in document

To add the Statement of Cash Flows:

  1. Locate where you want to add the statement of Cash Flows.

    2. You can add the statement of Cash Flows in an area or a note or a note heading.

  2. Go to the left side and select Add() to add new content.

  3. Choose Statement of Cash Flows from the list of content types.

    4. The Statement of Cash Flows populates in the document.

Note: You can add the Statement of Cash Flows in your financial statements document only once. If you want to move the Statement of Cash Flows to a different location in the document, delete it from the document and add it again in the new location.

To delete the statement of Cash Flows:

  1. Select the Statement of Cash Flows.

  2. Select More actions () | Delete.
