Navegar por unidades de negocio

If your firm utilizes the business units feature, you can navigate between business units and their entities using the following methods:

  • On login, you will automatically connect to your last accessed entity in its respective business unit.

  • You can switch between available business units from the Cloud menu ().

  • You can search for entities across all business units using the entity selector next to the Cloud menu. Each entity will also display its parent business unit.

    If you select an entity in another business unit, you’ll switch to that business unit automatically.

Note that staff can only access business units where they’ve been assigned. For information on assigning staff to business units, see Manage staff across business units.
